Single Sign-On (SSO) lets your team use your identity provider to access June without managing separate passwords.

## What you need

- A verified email domain in June (`Settings > Email Domains`)
- Identity provider admin rights (currently Okta is supported)

## Set up SSO with Okta

Follow the Okta-specific steps to enable SSO for your workspace.

- Read the guide: [Okta SSO setup](/authentication/sso-setup)
