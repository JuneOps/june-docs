## Advanced Capabilities

### AI-Driven Asset Management
- **Predictive Analytics**: Anticipate device failures and maintenance needs
- **Smart Recommendations**: Get actionable insights for optimization
- **Automated Workflows**: Reduce manual tasks with intelligent automation

### Enterprise Integration
- **MDM Platforms**: Jamf Pro, Microsoft Entra ID (Intune), Kandji
- **Identity Providers**: Okta, Google Workspace, Azure AD, SAML/SCIM
- **Custom APIs**: Extend June's capabilities with robust API access

### Comprehensive Reporting
- **Real-time Dashboards**: Monitor your device fleet at a glance
- **Custom Reports**: Build reports tailored to your organization's needs
- **Compliance Tracking**: Ensure adherence to security and policy requirements
