Welcome to June! This guide will help you connect your systems and start managing your device fleet in under 15 minutes.

## What you'll accomplish
- Connect your MDM system to import device data
- Set up identity provider integration for user management
- View your complete asset inventory
- Understand your device health at a glance

## Step 1: Initial Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access June">
    Navigate to your June instance and log in with your administrator credentials.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Complete Profile Setup">
    Fill out your profile information and set your preferences for notifications and time zones.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 4: View Your Asset Inventory

Now that your systems are connected, explore your complete device inventory.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Your Dashboard">
    Return to the main dashboard to see your device overview with real data from your MDM system.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Explore Asset Categories">
    Review the different device categories automatically discovered:
    - **Laptops**: MacBooks, Windows laptops, Chromebooks
    - **Desktops**: iMacs, Windows PCs, workstations
    - **Mobile Devices**: iPhones, iPads, Android devices
    - **Specialized Equipment**: Monitors, accessories, and other IT assets
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Check Device Health">
    June automatically analyzes your device fleet to show:
    - **Health Status**: Overall device condition and performance
    - **Compliance**: Policy adherence and security posture
    - **Update Status**: Operating system and application updates
    - **Usage Patterns**: Device utilization and performance metrics
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Try Smart Search">
    Use June's natural language search to find specific devices:
    - "Show me all MacBooks that need updates"
    - "Find devices assigned to the Sales team"
    - "List all Windows laptops with low battery health"
    
    <Tip>
    Save frequently used searches as custom views for quick access later.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 5: Explore AI Insights

June's AI analyzes your device data to provide actionable insights.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Review AI Recommendations">
    Navigate to **AI Features > AI Insights** to see:
    - Devices at risk of hardware failure
    - Optimization opportunities for better performance
    - Security recommendations and compliance gaps
    - Cost-saving suggestions for device refresh cycles
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Set Up Automated Alerts">
    Configure notifications for:
    - Critical device health issues
    - Compliance violations
    - Upcoming refresh recommendations
    - Security alerts requiring attention
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Need Help?

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Integration Issues">
    If you're having trouble connecting your MDM or identity provider:
    - Verify your API credentials and permissions
    - Check our [troubleshooting guide](/troubleshooting/integration-issues)
    - Contact support with your specific error messages
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="No Devices Showing">
    If devices aren't appearing after MDM integration:
    - Wait 15-30 minutes for initial sync to complete
    - Verify your MDM system has devices enrolled
    - Check the integration sync status in Settings
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="User Access Issues">
    If team members can't access June:
    - Ensure SSO is properly configured
    - Verify user groups are mapped to appropriate roles
    - Check user provisioning settings in your identity provider
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Note>
Complete setup typically takes 15-30 minutes. Once your integrations are running, June will continuously sync data and provide real-time insights into your device fleet.
</Note>
