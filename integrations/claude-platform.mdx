If you're on a claude.ai Enterprise plan, connect [Claude Enterprise](/integrations/claude-enterprise) instead. It gives billed, per-person spend across every product (see [Why Enterprise is better](#why-enterprise-is-better) below).

## What you'll accomplish
- Connect with your Anthropic **Admin API key**
- See your **billed** Claude bill at the organization level (by workspace and model)
- See **estimated** per-person **Claude Code** usage

## What June syncs

From your Anthropic Console, June syncs:

- Your **billed organization total**, broken down by **workspace** and **model**. This is your authoritative invoice total. It is **not** attributed to individual people.
- An **estimated per-user cost for Claude Code only**.

Usage history is available from January 2026 onward and refreshes daily.

<Warning>
On the Platform connection, per-person spend is **estimated** and covers **Claude Code only**. It is Anthropic's per-user *estimate*, not a billed figure, and it excludes Claude chat and other products. Your organization total is billed and authoritative, but it cannot be broken down per person. For **billed**, per-person spend across **all** Claude products, connect [Claude Enterprise](/integrations/claude-enterprise) instead.
</Warning>

## Capabilities

This is a read-only spend integration. June ingests usage and billing data, and does not take actions in Anthropic.

| Capability | Description |
|------------|-------------|
| **Billed spend** | Your authoritative Anthropic invoice total over the selected period, net of account credits. |
| **Spend over time** | Cumulative billed spend/tokens, by model, across the period. |
| **Per-workspace & per-model breakdowns** | Where your billed org spend concentrates. |
| **Estimated per-person Claude Code usage** | Anthropic's per-user estimate for Claude Code, clearly labeled as estimated and never summed on top of the billed total. |

## Prerequisites

Before starting, ensure you have:
- An Anthropic **Console** account with access to **Admin keys**

## Connect Claude Platform

<Steps>
  <Step title="Generate an Admin API key">
    1. Open the [Claude Console](https://platform.claude.com).
    2. Go to **Settings → Admin keys**.
    3. Create a key (it starts with `sk-ant-admin…`) and copy it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add the integration in June">
    1. In June, open the **Integrations** page.
    2. Choose **Claude Platform**.
    3. Paste your **Admin API key** and save.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for the first sync">
    June backfills your historical usage and refreshes daily. Spend appears under **AI usage → Claude** once the first sync completes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Why Enterprise is better

| | **Claude Platform** (this page) | **Claude Enterprise** |
|---|---|---|
| **Org total** | Billed (by workspace/model) | Billed (per user, summed) |
| **Per-person spend** | Estimated, **Claude Code only** | **Billed**, **all products** |
| **Reconciles to your bill per person** | No (estimated, partial) | Yes |

If you have access to a claude.ai Enterprise plan, connect [Claude Enterprise](/integrations/claude-enterprise) for billed, per-person spend across every Claude product.
