## Device Health Insights

### Predictive Health Monitoring

June's AI predicts potential device issues before they occur:

#### Hardware Failure Prediction
- **Battery Health**: Predicts when device batteries will need replacement
- **Storage Issues**: Identifies devices approaching storage capacity limits
- **Performance Degradation**: Detects declining performance trends
- **Component Failures**: Anticipates hardware component failures

#### Software Health Analysis
- **Update Compliance**: Identifies devices at risk of security vulnerabilities
- **Application Issues**: Detects problematic software installations
- **Configuration Drift**: Identifies devices deviating from standard configurations
- **Performance Impact**: Analyzes software impact on device performance

## Compliance Reports and Monitoring

### Automated Compliance Analysis

June's AI continuously monitors compliance across multiple dimensions:

#### Security Compliance
- **Patch Management**: Tracks OS and application update status
- **Encryption Status**: Monitors device encryption compliance
- **Antivirus Protection**: Verifies security software status
- **Access Control**: Analyzes user access and permissions

#### Policy Compliance
- **Configuration Compliance**: Compares device settings to organizational policies
- **Usage Policy Adherence**: Monitors compliance with acceptable use policies
- **Data Protection**: Ensures data handling compliance
- **Audit Requirements**: Tracks compliance with regulatory requirements

### Compliance Insights Dashboard

The AI-powered compliance dashboard provides:

- **Compliance Score**: Overall organizational compliance rating
- **Trend Analysis**: Compliance trends over time
- **Risk Assessment**: Potential compliance risks and their impact
- **Remediation Recommendations**: Specific actions to improve compliance

### Setting Up Compliance Monitoring

<Steps>
  <Step title="Define Compliance Policies">
    1. Navigate to Security & Compliance > Compliance Policies
    2. Define or import your organization's compliance requirements
    3. Set compliance thresholds and acceptable risk levels
    4. Configure compliance monitoring frequency
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Configure Monitoring Rules">
    Set up automated monitoring for:
    - Security patch compliance
    - Configuration standard adherence
    - Data protection compliance
    - Access control compliance
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Set Alert Thresholds">
    Configure alerts for:
    - Compliance score drops below threshold
    - Critical compliance violations
    - Trending compliance issues
    - Regulatory deadline approaching
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Establish Reporting Schedule">
    Set up automated compliance reporting:
    - Daily operational reports
    - Weekly trend summaries
    - Monthly executive dashboards
    - Quarterly compliance audits
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Acting on AI Recommendations

### Recommendation Types

June provides different types of AI-powered recommendations:

#### Device Management Recommendations
- **Refresh Planning**: Optimal timing for device replacements
- **Configuration Changes**: Suggested policy or setting adjustments
- **Performance Optimization**: Actions to improve device performance
- **Cost Management**: Opportunities to reduce device-related costs

#### User Experience Recommendations
- **Training Opportunities**: Suggested user education based on usage patterns
- **Workflow Improvements**: Process optimizations to enhance productivity
- **Tool Recommendations**: Software or hardware suggestions for specific users
- **Support Interventions**: Proactive support recommendations

### Implementing Recommendations

<Steps>
  <Step title="Review Recommendations">
    1. Access the AI Insights dashboard
    2. Review recommended actions and their priority levels
    3. Understand the rationale behind each recommendation
    4. Assess the potential impact and resource requirements
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Prioritize Actions">
    Prioritize recommendations based on:
    - Business impact and urgency
    - Resource availability
    - Risk reduction potential
    - Cost-benefit analysis
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Create Action Plans">
    For each recommendation:
    - Assign responsible team members
    - Set implementation timelines
    - Define success metrics
    - Plan communication strategy
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Track Implementation">
    Monitor progress on:
    - Recommendation implementation status
    - Measured impact of implemented changes
    - User feedback and satisfaction
    - Follow-up actions needed
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Customizing AI Preferences

### Adjusting AI Sensitivity

Customize how June's AI analyzes your environment:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access AI Settings">
    Navigate to Administration > AI & Insights > Settings
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Configure Analysis Parameters">
    Adjust settings for:
    - Alert sensitivity levels
    - Prediction time horizons
    - Trend analysis periods
    - Anomaly detection thresholds
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Set Organizational Context">
    Provide context to improve AI accuracy:
    - Business priorities and goals
    - Risk tolerance levels
    - Compliance requirements
    - Operational constraints
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Define Exclusions">
    Specify devices or scenarios to exclude:
    - Test/development devices
    - Special use cases
    - Temporary configurations
    - Known acceptable anomalies
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Measuring AI Impact

### Key Performance Indicators

Track the effectiveness of AI insights:

- **Mean Time to Detection (MTTD)**: How quickly issues are identified
- **Mean Time to Resolution (MTTR)**: How quickly issues are resolved
- **Prediction Accuracy**: Percentage of accurate predictions
- **Recommendation Adoption**: Rate of AI recommendation implementation
- **Cost Savings**: Quantifiable savings from AI-driven optimizations
